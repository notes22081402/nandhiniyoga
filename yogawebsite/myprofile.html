Beginner Yoga


Perfect for newcomers, this class introduces the fundamentals of yoga, focusing on basic poses, proper alignment, and breathing techniques.

Vinyasa Flow


A dynamic and flowing practice that connects breath with movement. Vinyasa Flow is ideal for those looking to build heat, endurance, and flexibility.

Hatha Yoga


A gentle practice that emphasizes posture and breath, suitable for all levels. Hatha yoga helps build strength, flexibility, and mindfulness.

Power Yoga


An intense and fast-paced class designed to build strength and stamina. Power Yoga is perfect for those who want a challenging workout.

Restorative Yoga


A calming practice using props to support the body in restful postures. Restorative Yoga is excellent for stress relief and deep relaxation.

Yin Yoga


A slow-paced style of yoga with postures held for longer periods. Yin Yoga targets deep connective tissues and promotes relaxation and healing.
